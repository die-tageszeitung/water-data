Did the money spending change over time?

Annotations on possible directions / leads

Funding is constant over years, with an overall positive trend. We see interesting negative outliers for 1993, 2003 and 2016. The positive trend doesn't interest only the quantity of allocated funds, but also the absolute numbers of projects in place. We have a lot of data points with little or no information on the year.

Does the funding strategy change over time?

Annotations on possible directions / leads

The funding strategies changes over time. Public corporations are always prominent. Initially we have smaller and fewer projects, in the years the number of projects grow as well as the total amount of money allocated for them. Some countries receive money either smaller and larger funds, while some others are capable of getting only very limited amount of money.
